Official Biography

Sanjay Shangloo

Singer • Music Director • Lyricist • Founder of Sa Re Gao Re Records

A renowned Indian singer and disciple of Anup Jalota, Sanjay Shangloo is celebrated for soulful bhajans, ghazals, Sufi music and Bollywood melodies.

With releases on major labels and performances across India and worldwide, he continues to inspire audiences while nurturing emerging talent.

About Sanjay Shangloo

Sanjay Shangloo is a renowned Indian singer, music director, lyricist and music entrepreneur based in Mumbai, India. Known for his soulful voice, classical training and emotionally rich performances, he has established a distinctive identity in the Indian music industry.

Disciple of Anup Jalota

A dedicated disciple of Bhajan Samrat Anup Jalota, Sanjay Shangloo has inherited a deep understanding of Indian classical music and devotional expression. He has also shared the stage with his mentor at numerous prestigious events.

Professional Career

Sanjay has built a successful career as a singer, music director, lyricist, composer, live performer, music producer and mentor. His versatile repertoire includes Bhajans, Ghazals, Sufi music, devotional songs and Bollywood melodies.

Major Music Labels

  • T-Series
  • Ultra Music
  • N.A. Classical Awaaz Music
  • Sanjay Shangloo Music
  • Sa Re Gao Re Records

Founder of Sa Re Gao Re Records

Sanjay Shangloo is the founder of Sa Re Gao Re Records, an independent music label dedicated to discovering and promoting singers, lyricists, composers and independent artists.

Live Show Performances

He is available for corporate events, weddings, devotional programs, cultural festivals, Bhajan Sandhya, Jagran, Ghazal Nights and private concerts across India and internationally.

Digital Presence

With over 100,000 followers on Facebook and a growing audience on Instagram and YouTube, Sanjay Shangloo continues to connect with music lovers worldwide.
